Classic Cheese Burger Recipe

a

Ingredients

  • Two Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Two Beef patty
  • Any amount of Pickles
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Pepper/Optional
  • Two Buns
  • Bacon/Optional
  • Tomato
    • y

    Steps

    1. 1. Gather Ingridients
    2. 2. Shape the patties into a circular Shape
    3. 3. Warm the pan at medium heat for 2 minutes
    4. 4. Brush the buns with butter gently
    5. 5. Toast the buns at medium heat for 3 minutes
    6. 6. Now cook your two patties for 8 to 9 minutes at 160 (F)/medium heat, with your cheese at the last minute.
    7. 7. Add mayo on your top inner bun, you can add mayo to the bottom bun if you'd like.
    8. 8. I would reccomend 4 pickles but you can use more
    9. 9. Now add a tad bit of ketchup
    10. 10. Add two slics of thin cut peppers.
    11. 11. Let it settle for a bit and you are done.
